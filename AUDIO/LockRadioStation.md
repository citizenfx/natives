## LOCK_RADIO_STATION

```c
// 0x477D9DB48F889591
void LOCK_RADIO_STATION(char* radioStationName, BOOL toggle);
```

This disables the radio station completely - it won't be selectable on the radio wheel or ever be heard coming from a vehicle/ambient emitter
